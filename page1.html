The Research and Experimentation Process

After choosing our topic, we decided to get a broad view on the whole topic in order to decide on our future plan and area of focus. With the recent announcement of the French government (at the the time) to invest 300M euros in Hydrogen vehicle infrastructure to increase their development, we were inspired: we would focus on Hydrogen cars today-what is the current state of these vehicles, and what can be done to improve them in the future. So, after months of experiments and research, our hard work resulted in this website!
